Evening's Embr

The day has softened into gold,
A gentle light that slowly fades,
And in this quiet hour behold,
Your love, a flame that never wanes.

When shadows stretch and thoughts grow deep,
And doubts like whispers start to creep,
Remember: you are never alone—
A thousand suns within you glow.

So rest now in this evening's hold,
And let the night its peace impart.
The world may spin, may grow cold,
But you have strength to mend each broken part.

For in your heart, a fire burns
That time and tide cannot erase.
Every end a new turn,
And every step, love's sweet embrace.

So go now, brave and undeterred,
With hope as your companion dear.
By morning's light, you'll be heard—
Your spirit bright, your soul sincere.
